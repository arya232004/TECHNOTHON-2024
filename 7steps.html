Welcome to Technothon 2024 - A 24-hours Hackathon exclusively for Polytechnic Students. Building on the success of our inaugural event in 2023, Technothon continues to be the epicenter of innovation for the engineering community across Mumbai.

In 2023, we witnessed remarkable creativity and problem-solving skills during our 24-hour nonstop hackathon. The overwhelming response fueled our commitment to providing an even more exhilarating experience in 2024.

At Technothon, our aim is to provide a platform for students across the city to inculcate invaluable skills in product development. By building projects from scratch and competing with fellow mates, participants identify and hone their innovation and problem-solving skills.

7 step Process
At Technothon 2024, we've streamlined the journey from ideation to implementation with our 7-step process. This structured approach ensures a seamless and rewarding experience for all participants:

Team Formation
Collaborate and form your team, laying the foundation for a strong and diverse group.
Brainstorming & Problem Statement Selection
Engage in brainstorming sessions to select a challenge aligned with your team's strengths and interests.
Registration & Idea Submission
Register your team using the provided registration form and submit your innovative idea. Let your creativity shine through in a presentation format, using the template provided.
Shortlisting Contender
Teams with the most promising ideas move forward to the Grand Finale, a real-time testing ground for their innovative solutions.
The Grand Finale - 24 hours Hackathon
Immerse yourself in the electrifying atmosphere of a 24-hour live hackathon. Collaborate, code, and bring your idea to life.
Project Presentation
Showcase your implementation to our esteemed judges. Demonstrate the productiveness of your concept and the impact it can make.
Winner Declaration
The moment of triumph arrives as winners are announced. Efforts are recognized with prize money and a sense of accomplishment at the closing ceremony.

Navigate this process with determination, innovation, and teamwork. Your journey at Technothon 2024 is not just about coding; it's about turning ideas into impactful solutions
